BANK
An independent grid with its own row buffer. DDR4 chips have 16 banks, DDR5 has 32 so many rows can be open at once, in different banks, in parallel.
CHANNEL
A whole separate pathway to the controller. More channels = more lanes of traffic each channel can transfer data completely independently.
RANK
A group of chips that all respond together on the same channel. Ranks time-share the channel but they can prepare in the background.
